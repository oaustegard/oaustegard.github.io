Claude Pruner

Selectively prune and export Claude.ai conversations — messages, artifacts, tool usage, and thinking steps.

Granular Selection Toggle individual messages, tool calls, and thinking blocks on or off.
Bulk Controls Quickly toggle all human, assistant, tool, or thinking content at once.
Copy & Download Export your pruned conversation as structured text to clipboard or file.
Token Estimates Live word count and estimated token usage as you select content.
Get the Bookmarklet
All processing happens locally in your browser — no data is sent to any server.

How It Works

  1. Install the companion bookmarklet in your browser.
  2. Open any conversation on claude.ai and click the bookmarklet.
  3. This page opens with your conversation broken into its components.
  4. Click items to deselect them, use bulk toggles, then copy or download the result.
